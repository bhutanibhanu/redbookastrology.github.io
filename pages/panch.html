PANCHANG

A panchang is a Hindu calendar and almanac, which follows traditional units of Hindu timekeeping, and presents important dates and their calculations in a tabulated form

The five integral aspects of Panchang : Tithi(Date) calculated as per the state of the moon during the waxing and waning phase. Nakshatra (Group of Stars) calculated based on in which constellation the moon is journeying at the moment . Yoga (an auspicious time) Karam (Half of Tithi ) Vaar (Seven days of the week ) .
